Adobe Illustrator

This logo was created for my fictional company "Finding Fido", which was created to help people find local pet-friendly places in the Harrisonburg area. I wanted to emphasize a more lighthearted and endearing tone, so I decided to design a dog mascot and also chose a more playful font to help convey that.

This logo was designed for a student entrepreneur's product "Boost", described as a juice-based serotonin boosting drink. Using the description as inspiration, I took the serotonin molecule and incorporated it into the name of the product itself, creating a cohesive and clever brand identity.

This logo was created for a hand-coded website for exploring Harrisonburg and showcasing what it has to offer. The logo is simple but clever, with the words and imagery being interwoven to express the city's natural beauty.

Adobe InDesign

These two spreads focus in on an article written about the Hong Kong Umbrella Revolution, showcasing my abilities to create more serious and dramatic pieces. I chose to keep the color palette black and white with the accent color being yellow - the Umbrella Revolution's official color.
